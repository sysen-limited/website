Types of account

There are only two types of accounts those who have a subscription and those who do not have a subscription.

Subscription accounts have a commitment to the current term they are signed up for, however that commitment is only for the base package any additional features are then only charged for while activated.

Included features for either option

Every account gains access to the following features, there are no restrictions depending on your account type, once signed up you can start using them all.

Personalisation Included
  • Store name and logo
  • Select store colour scheme
  • Banner images
 Product listings Included
  • Product images
  • Descriptions of products
  • Pricing with "retail" and "sale" pricing
 Customer checkout Included
  • Linked with our payment gateway
  • Create customer accounts
  • Capture shipping and billing information
 Order management Included
  • Keep customers up to date during order fulfilment
  • Attach shipping information
  • Full order history and searching abilities
 Analytics Coming soon (Q2 2018)

Subscription requests

We are always listening to feedback and value your ideas. Recommend something you would like to see and if it fits with our product vision we will contact you.

If we implement a feature you recommend we will credit your account with up to 12 months of your subscription covered, even if your idea has been recommended by others.
