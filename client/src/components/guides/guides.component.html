Getting started

Registration is quick and easy, just provide your email address and we will send a registration message to you. This is so we can ensure your account belongs to you.

Confirmation of your account is done via a link in this email and requires setting up a password to login with.

Activate your store allowing customers to place orders by providing your bank account details to our payment gateway. We will send you weekly payments for any purchases made on your store after any fees incurred.

Now you can customise your store and upload products to sell. Choose a name for your store for free using our domain giving you a presence on the internet.

Take a look at our live demo store at https://demo.venzra.io
