Getting started

It is as simple as 1, 2, 3 to get your store up and running, check out our guides section for more information or contact us.

1

Register and setup store

Creating an account is easy, just provide your email address and follow the instructions in the email we send you. Then add details on how you want to be recieve payouts and setup any custom information about your store via our admin portal.

2

Market your products

Once you have setup your core account information add the products you want to sell on your store. You can provide images and descriptions about your products, and with a subscription you can also manage stock details.

3

Manage consumer sales

Your store is online and customers can begin making purchases. But to reach your potential you will need to advertise, we support effective SEO options, however there are many ways today to advertise your online store and we can help recommend some tried and tested methods.
