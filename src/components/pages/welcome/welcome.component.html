Discover a safer way to protect your customers

It is important for a modern website to have modern security practices. Cyber-security is a constantly changing goal, we work with standards and best practices with Venzra. Frequent release cycles mean when a vulnerability is found either with our software or the technologies used it will be patched in the shortest time possible.

Here are a few of the ways we are doing this

SSL-Certificates

All our customers get an HTTPS (SSL-Certificate) with TLS 1.2 for their domains at no extra cost.

PCI-DSS

We are compliant with industry standards for security this includes the latest PCI-DSS v3.2.

OWASP Top-10

This is a set of common practices used by hackers, we design from the ground up to protect from these.

Encryption

Sensitive data, such as user passwords are encrypted with one-way algorithms that cannot be decoded.
