About us

We are breaking down the technical barrier.

Venzra is on a mission to make it easy for companies to do business online, we are starting with simple transactions but moving on towards bigger things, join us early as we build on this vision.

June 2017

Our Journey Begins

After a few years of research and several project pivots we decided to build Venzra, the strategy solidified and product development was locked in.

January 2018

Venzra launches into private Alpha

The first cut of our platform launches, with the core functionality working. This phase was all about gathering feedback and making rapid changes with a small user base.

June 2018

Official launch of Venzra into Beta

We formally launch Venzra as an eCommerce platform at a local business exhibition. The Surrey Business Expo, Guildford, UK. We are now open for business and ready to help other businesses grow online.

November 2018 (the future)

Venzra is invited to WebSummit in Lisbon

Following the acceptance of our solution as a viable technology Startup for the eCommerce industry, we are exhibiting along with the top technology businesses in Europe to introduce Venzra to a wider audience.
